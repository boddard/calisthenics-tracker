How to use
Everything you need to run a competition from your phone
Before competition starts
1
Add your teams
Go to the Teams tab and add every team competing. If a club is entering both an A and B team, tick the "This team has a B team" checkbox before saving — this unlocks a separate B entry in the running order.
2
Set the running order
Go to the Order tab. Select each event using the pills at the top, then tap teams from the pool to add them in the order they will perform. Use the ↑↓ buttons to adjust. Teams with a B team appear as two separate entries — add them independently wherever they appear in the running order.
During the competition
3
Enter scores as they're announced
Go to the Enter tab. Select the event, then pick the competitor number — this matches the order each team performed, so #1 performed first, #2 second, and so on. Type in the raw score and tap Save score.
4
Watch the leaderboard update
Tap Standings to see the live leaderboard. Scores appear immediately. Placings and points are only calculated once every competing team has a score for that event — until then, a ? is shown.
5
Fix a mistake
Go to the Enter tab and scroll down to the entered scores log. Tap × next to any score to remove it, then re-enter the correct value.
Scoring rules
Placing points
1st6 points
2nd4 points
3rd2 points
HM1 point — Highly Commended
HC0.5 points — Honorable Mention
Ties
Teams with equal raw scores share the same placing and both receive full points. The next place below is still awarded to the next team — no places are skipped.
A and B teams
Where a team enters both an A and B squad in the same event, only the higher raw score counts toward that team's total and placing. The lower score is ignored entirely.
Trophy winner
The overall winner is the team with the most placing points across all events. The leaderboard is sorted by points at all times.
Data & privacy
Your data stays on your device
All teams, running orders, and scores are saved locally in your browser. Nothing is sent to any server. A refresh will not lose your data — but clearing your browser data or using a different device will start fresh.
